Superman - The Man of Steel

Biography

Superman, also known as Kal-El, is an alien from the planet Krypton who possesses superhuman abilities. As the last son of Krypton, he was sent to Earth by his parents before the destruction of his home planet. Raised by the Kents in Smallville, Clark learned the values of compassion, humility, and justice. Superman’s dual identity as Clark Kent allows him to relate to humanity, even as he stands as Earth’s greatest protector. His unwavering sense of hope and justice makes him a beacon of inspiration for people around the world.

Fun Facts

  • Superman’s alter ego, Clark Kent, works as a reporter for the Daily Planet.
  • He is vulnerable to Kryptonite, a mineral from his home planet.

Enemy List

Lex Luthor

Superman’s arch-nemesis, a genius billionaire with a vendetta against the Man of Steel.

Brainiac

A brilliant alien android who seeks to shrink cities and collect them, including Superman’s Metropolis.

General Zod

A Kryptonian general with powers similar to Superman’s.

Abilities

  • Superhuman strength, speed, and durability
  • Heat vision, X-ray vision, and freeze breath
  • Flight and super agility
  • Invulnerability (except to Kryptonite)

Important Relationships

  • Jimmy Olsen – Jimmy is Superman’s close friend and photographer at the Daily Planet. Their friendship goes beyond professional ties, and Jimmy has been an ally in Superman’s most dangerous battles.
  • Lois Lane – Lois Lane, Superman’s love interest, and a key figure in his life. She represents everything Clark Kent holds dear about humanity and has stood by him through his challenges.
  • Supergirl (Kara Zor-El) – Kara Zor-El, Superman’s cousin, shares similar Kryptonian powers and has often been a valuable ally to Superman. Their family bond strengthens their teamwork in protecting Earth.
